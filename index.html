Fire, Smoke & Fusion

Black Stag Fusion BBQ is a roaming food truck bringing slow-smoked meats, Latin street-food comfort, and bright Asian heat to North Georgia breweries, patios, and late-night hangs.

Expect loaded tacos, rice bowls, stacked sliders, crispy wedges, and rotating specials built around Korean-inspired scratch sauces.
